About Us:

At PandaPalate we're passionate about cooking. Our mission is to help teach people how to cook healthy, delicious and easy to make meals. Our cooking lessons cover a broad range and are available to everyone, from private or public lessons, we're here to transform your kitchen into an Italian restaurant.

Our Story

Luca Smith, a passionate and driven chef, wanting to share his experience and love for the arts of cooking teammed up with Aria Patel to create a platform where people of all ages can learn the basics of cooking.
We believe that there is no better time to start cooking, then now.
We wanted to also provide a range of foods, from healthy to fatty and food that also fits into dietary requirements. We always had a struggle to find food for different times in our day, so our recipes are specifically for anytime, lunch, breakfast, dinner or even for a late night snack, our food is for all your needs.
